Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information TaloNet collects through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect as little as we need to respond to you.

1 — Who we are

TaloNet (“TaloNet,” “we,” “us”) is the controller of the personal information described in this policy. For privacy questions, contact privacy@talonet.example.

2 — What we collect

CategoryExamples
Briefing requestsName, organization, work email, country / jurisdiction, and the message you send us.
CorrespondenceEmails and other communications you send to us.
Technical dataBasic server and security logs (such as IP address and request metadata) generated when you visit, kept for security and reliability.

This site does not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies, and we do not sell personal information. Fonts and media are self-hosted rather than loaded from third-party networks.

3 — How we use it

  • To respond to your briefing, evaluation, or procurement enquiry and to communicate with you about it.
  • To screen enquiries for eligibility, jurisdiction, and export-control or sanctions compliance.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website.
  • To comply with legal obligations and to establish or defend legal claims.

Our legal bases (where applicable) are your consent, our legitimate interest in responding to qualified enquiries and securing the site, and compliance with legal obligations.

4 — Sharing

We share personal information only with service providers who host the site and our email/communications on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations, and where required by law or to comply with export-control and sanctions screening. We do not sell or rent personal information.

5 — Retention

We keep briefing-request information for as long as needed to respond and to maintain a record of the enquiry, then delete or anonymize it. Security logs are kept for a limited period. We will return or delete information sooner on valid request, subject to legal retention requirements.

6 — Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security; please do not send classified, controlled, or operationally sensitive details through the website form.

7 — International transfers

We may process information in countries other than your own. Where we transfer personal information across borders, we use appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

8 — Your choices & rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, or to object or withdraw consent. To exercise any of these, contact privacy@talonet.example. You can also ask us at any time to stop contacting you.

9 — Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the “last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be posted on this page.
